Welcome to Client Communications
Everything you need to stay in sync with your clients — from listing signed to deal closed. Here's how it works.
⚡
Start with Action Required
The top of your dashboard tells you exactly what's due and for which listing. Red = overdue. Amber = due today. Blue = next step. Start there every time you log in.
📋
Add context, then generate
Each communication has pre-filled fields from your listing data. Add your notes, feedback, and next steps — the more context you give, the better the draft. You can always edit before sending.
✉️
Always check To / From / CC
Before hitting Send, review the email header — confirm the client address, your sending address, and which teammates are CC'd. Emails send directly from your Gmail account.
📬
It sends from your Gmail
When you send from this tool, the email goes out from your Gmail account and will appear in your Gmail Sent folder — just like any email you send manually.
Help & Quick Reference
Getting Started
▼
  • The Action Required queue at the top shows what's due across all your listings — start here every session.
  • Click any listing card to open its full communication timeline and send panel.
  • Switch between team members at the top to draft on behalf of a colleague.
💡 Red = overdue · Amber = due today · Blue = next step
Sending a Communication
▶
  • Select the communication type from the right panel or Action Needed box.
  • Fill in any context fields — the more you add, the better the draft.
  • Click Generate Draft — the AI will draft the email using your inputs and SIG tone standards.
  • Always review and edit the draft before sending. Use the toolbar for formatting.
  • Check the To / From / CC / Subject fields before hitting Send.
  • After sending, check your Gmail Sent folder — it will appear there.
Bi-Weekly Update
▶
  • Pull your inquiry and LOI counts from ReThink before starting.
  • Add broker notes, buyer feedback, and next steps for the best output.
  • The email is a short summary. A full-detail PDF report is automatically generated and attached when you send.
  • The PDF uses SIG brand formatting and downloads to your computer to simulate the attachment.
💡 The PDF auto-downloads when you send — check your Downloads folder.
Listing Extension
▶
  • Before opening the extension compose screen, have your amendment document ready.
  • Upload the amendment PDF or paste a DocuSign link on the first screen — this helps the AI reference it in the email.
  • An amendment generator is coming to SIGMA in a future release. For now, generate it separately and upload here.
💡 No amendment yet? Paste your DocuSign link and the email will reference it automatically.
Navigation
▶
  • Active Listings tab — your deal team's listings with status and next action.
  • Communication History tab — searchable log of everything sent, plus upcoming scheduled e-blasts from SIG Marketing.
  • Click ← All Listings from a detail view to return to the dashboard.
  • The Estimated Timeline panel on each listing shows target dates for each communication step based on your go-live date.
